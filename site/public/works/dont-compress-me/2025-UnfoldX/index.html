Don't Compress Me: Unfold X

Installation, Culture Station Seoul 284, 2025

Overview

Interactive installation presented at the Unfold X 2025. Participants stood in front of a camera and moved freely for 10 seconds. More than 1000 people participated this time. Many group sessions were recorded.
