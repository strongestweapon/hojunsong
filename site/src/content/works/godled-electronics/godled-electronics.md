GODLED Electronics is Hojun Song's company for mass-producing God-shaped LED electronics. The idea began in 2010; the first prototypes appeared in 2016. 

Current lineup: Buddha (LG-BUD-6036), Jesus (LW-JES-7620), Mary (LB-MAR-4125), and Ganesh (LR-GAN-6036), with production planned for Q3 2021.
